The flow

From "where can we meet?" to confirmed in three moves.

No more chasing facilities over email. VenU collapses discovery, request and approval into one calm, trackable flow.

01
Discover the space

1Discover

Filter 240+ spaces by capacity, building, amenities and live availability. Every venue shows a real-time status dot — open, filling, or fully booked — so you never request a dead end.

2Request

Pick a date and time. VenU surfaces conflicts before you submit and suggests the nearest open slot. Add your purpose, attendee count and notes — then send it in one tap.

3Confirmed

Facilities approvers see your request in a clean queue with everything they need to decide. You get notified the moment it's approved, with the slot held the whole way through.

Why VenU

Quietly powerful, where it counts.

Every detail is built around the messy reality of shared campus space — overlapping requests, capacity limits and approval chains.

Live availability

Real-time status on every space. See what's open, filling or fully booked before you lift a finger.

Conflict detection

VenU catches overlaps the instant you pick a slot — and points you to the nearest free window.

Approvals that flow

A clean queue for facilities staff — approve, reject or comment with full context, in seconds.

Capacity-aware

Attendee counts are checked against each room's limit, so you never overbook a space.

Calendar at a glance

Your bookings, requests and holds laid out on one timeline you can actually read.

Role-based access

Students request, staff approve, admins manage the catalogue — everyone sees exactly what they should.
